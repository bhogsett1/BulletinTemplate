Greenspan 0-20 Lesson & Game    

Bruce Greenspan held another successful FREE lesson for any player with 0 - 20 points on Monday morning. Eight tables of players attended. Bruce hosted a FREE game Monday afternoon for any of those persons interested. Bruce Greenspan, a Floridian, is a renowned teacher, player and District 9 Board Member. He continually donates his time and efforts on behalf of exciting new players and welcoming them to the world of Duplicate Bridge.
